**Release candidate** — a pre-release build on the way to the open beta, not a public release.

### Added

- Replace the controller toggle with a live connection-status light and connection status with reachability auth and type

### Fixed

- Open the demo controller you picked in the first-run chooser
- Surface the gauge-style picker on compact temperature cards
- Pack two short dashboard cards side by side instead of stacking
- Drop the redundant inline Done button on the dashboard
- Auto-dismiss the control-action confirmation toast
- Accept relay writes POSTed in the form body
- Actually hide gauges hidden in the Measurements editor
- Collapse hidden Measurements cells instead of leaving empty space
- Re-enable R8 minification with verified keep rules
