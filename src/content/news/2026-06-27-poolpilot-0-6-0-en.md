What's new in 0.6.0

- Remote access to your pool — a profile can now hold a remote address alongside the local one, and the app switches between them automatically (local network first, remote when the controller isn't reachable at home). Violet uses its built-in remote-access feature; ProCon.IP has none, so it only works with your own setup (DynDNS, port forwarding, VPN or a reverse proxy)
- Timed relay switches — long-press a relay to switch it for a set duration, after which it returns to its previous setting (Auto/On/Off), with a custom-duration picker. Violet runs the timer on the controller, so it finishes even with the app closed; ProCon.IP runs it in the app — in the background on Android, but iOS needs to stay open (or you switch off from a notification)
- Manual dosing for ProCon.IP — trigger manual chlorine and pH dosing straight from the app, matching the controls Violet already offered
- Dashboard fixes — no longer goes blank after switching profiles, scrolls edge-to-edge behind the status bar, and falls back to a static background on Android devices that can't render the animated one
