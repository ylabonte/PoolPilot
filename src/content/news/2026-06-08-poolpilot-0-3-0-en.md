### What's new in 0.3.0

- Choose how many decimals each measurement shows — in the app, widgets and on your watch
- Demo profiles now open with a ready-made dashboard, so you can explore right away
- Pool cover controls now show live motion and status
- Numbers now follow your device's language and region everywhere
- Optional crash reporting to help us fix bugs faster (off by default)
- Plus accessibility, stability and visual polish
