PoolPilot 0.1.0 — our first public beta. One native app per platform that drives both the ProCon.IP and the Violet pool controllers (controller is chosen per profile).

**Highlights**
- Four-tab cockpit: Dashboard, Measurements, Switches, Settings.
- Live readings as banded gauges — pH, redox (ORP), chlorine, temperatures — with several gauge styles and adjustable ranges.
- Control relays, outputs and dosing (OFF / AUTO / ON), plus cover and Violet setpoints.
- Pin your most-used values and switches to the Dashboard — it starts empty, you decide what goes on it.
- Demo mode: explore both controllers fully offline, no hardware needed.
- Companions & glances: Apple Watch, Wear OS, home-screen widgets and (iOS) lock-screen complications.
- Per-profile backgrounds, auto-detect on connect, fully English + German.

**Known issues & limitations**
- This is an early beta — expect rough edges, and please report anything that looks off.
- Local network only: your phone must be on the same network as the controller (no remote/cloud access yet).
- Controllers talk over plain HTTP — you confirm this once before the first connection.
- If the app asks for a local-network permission, grant it — otherwise it can't reach the controller and connections time out.
- The Dashboard is empty until you pin items to it.
- The exact set of Pro features is still being refined during the beta.
