What's new in 0.5.0

- Trigger buttons for one-off actions — fire a Violet switching rule or a ProCon.IP digital input with a press-and-hold button, complete with haptic feedback
- Gauges that follow your controller — chemistry gauges now track the real warning bands and target values from your pool controller, and you can fine-tune any gauge's range right from the dashboard
- Save your targets to the controller — set pH, Redox (ORP) and temperature straight from the app
- Your own labels everywhere — Violet outputs now show the names you configured on the controller
- More polish all around — fuller VoiceOver/screen-reader and Dynamic Type support, a true-to-life in-app demo, and plenty of stability and layout fixes (Android can now pick a background photo from Settings, just like iOS)
